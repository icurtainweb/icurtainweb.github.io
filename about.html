Something about us

Curtain Know-It-Alls

We are New Zealand owned and operated. Our staff are experts in curtains and are here to help you find the perfect fabric and style to finish your room or just provide a bit of inspiration.

We have over 20 years of experience and in that time have seen many fashions come and go. Our buying team are always on the lookout for the next hot trend with frequent international trips. It is our aim to deliver the latest colours, designs and looks in custom made curtains and blinds as well as the most extensive range of readymade curtains in New Zealand. Our mission is to provide choice, quality and inspiration to the homes of New Zealand whether you're updating a single room or transforming an entire house. We're passionate about curtains.
